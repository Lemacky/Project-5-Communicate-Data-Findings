Investigation Overview¶

The goal of this presentation is to highlight key findings on this dataset.The target variable here is the value of players in the tournament.This presentation summarizes the major insights based on the relationship between the major independent variables and the dependent variable(Value of players).

Key Insights from The Dataset¶

  1. Most of the players in the dataset are from Europe, and majority of them are from England
  2. Players with normal body-type are more in the dataset, also most of the players in the datasets are right footed.
  3. There is a high positive correlation between players values and their wages
  4. Players values and wages have moderate positive correlation with international-reputation
  5. Majority of the right footed players are highly valuable and earns high wages
  6. More number of the Lean and Normal body-type players are highly valuable and earns high wages as compare to the Stocky players.
  7. Stocky players are the least popular in this dataset.But few Stocky players who are left footed are the most valuable and earns more than the normal and lean players.

Dataset Overview¶

This dataset consists of all the details of the players who participated in the FIFA World Cup 2019 and their final value at which they were to be sold to other clubs.Data can be found in https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/blurredmachine/fifa-2019-world-cup-dataset.
